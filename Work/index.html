⛵ Jaipur ➔ Ujjain ➔ Indore ➔ Omkareshwar Travel Plan

📅 Travel Dates

  • Start: 19th July 2025 (Saturday Evening)
  • Return: 23rd July 2025 (Wednesday)

🚂 Day 0: Jaipur to Ujjain

Departure Station: Jaipur Junction

Train: 12968 Jaipur - Chennai SF Express

  • Departure Time: ~19:00
  • Arrival in Ujjain: ~06:00 (next morning)

Tip: Book sleeper/3AC ticket in advance.

🌇 Day 1: Ujjain Sightseeing (20th July 2025)

✅ Places to Visit:

  • 🚩 Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga
  • ⚡ Bhasma Aarti (book online)
  • 🙏 Harsiddhi Temple
  • 🔮 Kal Bhairav Temple
  • 🌊 Ram Ghat (evening aarti)

🍽 Food to Try:

  • Ujjaini Poha
  • Kachori
  • Thandai

Stay: Near Mahakal temple (budget hotels)

🏨 Day 2: Travel to Indore + Indore Sightseeing (21st July 2025)

🚌 Travel: Ujjain to Indore (55 km)

  • Options: Train, Bus, or Cab (1.5 hrs)

🎭 Sightseeing:

  • Rajwada Palace
  • Lal Bagh Palace
  • Kanch Mandir
  • Chappan Dukan (lunch)
  • Sarafa Bazaar (night street food)

Stay: Palasia or Sarwate Bus Stand

🚤 Day 3: Omkareshwar Day Trip (22nd July 2025)

📍 Travel: Indore to Omkareshwar (80 km)

  • Taxi: ~2 hrs (~₹1,200)
  • Bus: From Sarwate Bus Stand (~2.5 hrs)

✅ Places to Visit:

  • Omkareshwar Jyotirlinga Temple
  • Mamleshwar Temple
  • Boat Ride on Narmada River
  • Om Parikrama Walk

Return: Reach Indore by 5 PM

🛄 Day 4: Return to Jaipur (23rd July 2025)

  • Train: 12967 Chennai - Jaipur SF Express
  • Departure: ~16:00 from Indore
  • Arrival in Jaipur: Morning of 24th July
